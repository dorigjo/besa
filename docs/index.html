Besa

The receipt layer for AI-agent actions.

AI agents call real systems — CRMs, payment APIs, deployment tools, databases. Every call is an action. Besa signs the admission decision and issues a tamper-evident receipt for every one. 

manifest.yaml
  ↓  besa sign         →  signed-manifest.json
  ↓  besa verify
  ↓  besa admit crm.lookup   →  ✓ admitted
  ↓  besa receipt crm.lookup →  receipt.json

The problem

AI agents are acting in real systems. Most teams cannot prove what was admitted, what was denied, or what was executed. There is no receipt.

The category

Agent Action Receipts.

A signed, tamper-evident record of an AI-agent tool call. It encodes the admission decision, the manifest hash, the request fingerprint, and a cryptographic signature. Not a log. Proof.

What a receipt proves
  • Which tool manifest was signed and by whom
  • Whether the tool call was admitted or denied
  • Which capability and risk level were declared
  • The cryptographic fingerprint of the request
  • The public key that signed the decision
