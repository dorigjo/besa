Besa

The receipt layer for AI-agent actions.

AI agents are calling real systems: CRMs, payment APIs, databases, deployment tools. The missing primitive is not another chatbot. It is proof — what was allowed, what was denied, what was executed, and under which signed grant.

The problem

AI agents are getting permissions to touch real systems. Most teams cannot prove what was decided — or why.

The primitive

Agent Action Receipts.

Besa signs tool manifests, evaluates admission, and issues a tamper-evident receipt for every decision — allow or deny.

Besa is the receipt layer for AI-agent actions.

What a receipt proves
  • which tool was called
  • by which agent
  • under which grant
  • whether it was allowed or denied
  • with a cryptographic signature
